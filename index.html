01 — About

I like problems with edges.

My background is in .NET and backend architecture — the kind of work where a wrong assumption doesn't show up until three services downstream. I care about systems that are simple enough to reason about, even as they grow.

Right now that means moving a bot platform serving elderly users — across a physical robot, a web client, and voice — off a legacy dialog framework and onto a modern multi-agent architecture. It's the kind of migration where the interesting decisions are about boundaries: what state lives where, what a "session" even means across four different channels.

Right now

  • Building A thin compatibility layer over Azure AI Foundry agents, so existing channels keep working during migration.
  • Learning Agentic architecture patterns, session/thread management, and how to test them properly.
  • Based in Catalonia, Spain
